Introduction

This document describes an application profile, in this case RRMV. The application profile specifies a semantic data model covering a set of usecases. The specification consists of terms with their constraints (cardinalities, codelists, usage guidelines).

This document describes the Regulatory Reporting Metadata Vocabulary that was developed in the context of the SORTIS initiative. The purpose of this vocabulary is to provide the minimum necessary concepts to describe a request, originating from a legal resource, the resulting action and several key concepts related to its execution.

Action

Definition
An event with at least one agent that is participant in it in a given time.
Properties
For this entity the following properties are defined: at time , has agent role , has annotation , has next , has result , has status , related to .

Property Expected Range Cardinality Definition Usage Codelist
[o] at time Period of Time 0..* An Action or a Role is valid within a time interval.
[o] has agent role Agent Role 0..* This property connects the executed Action to the Agent that was involved in its exectuion.
[o] has annotation xsd:string 0..* Annotation related to the Action or Request made by the domain expert.
[o] has next Action 0..* A property linking an Action with the succeeding Action in a repetitive sequence of actions generated by a Request.

Request

Definition
A portion of legal source expression including a mandate that produces at least one Action.
Properties
For this entity the following properties are defined: changes , cites , cites , has annotation , has topic , has unit , has URI , has value , is part of , produces , related to .
Add an issue for this entity here.
Property Expected Range Cardinality Definition Usage Codelist
[o] changes Request 0..* Indicates that this work or expression legally changes another. This encompasses the notions of amendment, replacement, repeal, or other types of change. This may be a direct change (textual or non-textual amendment) or a consequential or indirect change.
[o] cites Request 0..* Citation in the text of the legislation. This may be at the legal resource or legal expression level, as required by the implementation context. This includes verbatim citation and citations in referrals.
[o] cites Expression 0..* Citation in the text of the legislation. This may be at the legal resource or legal expression level, as required by the implementation context. This includes verbatim citation and citations in referrals.
[o] has annotation xsd:string 0..* Annotation related to the Action or Request made by the domain expert.
[o] has topic skos:Concept 0..* The topic of the request.
[o] has unit Frequency 0..* The property linking a Request with the Frequency and expressing the unit of this frequency.
[o] has URI xsd:anyURI 0..* URI of the Request.
[o] has value Frequency 0..* The property linking a Request a Frequency expressing the value of the unit of the frequency (e.g., 2 is the value, quarter is the unit).
[o] is part of Expression 0..* Indicates a work in which this one is physically included. Covers the case of text included in an Official Journal, or an article included in a text.
[o] produces Action 0..* A Request produces one or many Action.
[o] related to Request 0..* Indicates a somehow related other document, not necessarily a legal resource.
